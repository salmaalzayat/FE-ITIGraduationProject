  • How can I search for a specific doctor or specialist on the platform?

    To find a specific doctor or specialist, simply select the desired specialization and the preferred doctor, then proceed to search for their available appointments. Our platform allows you to tailor your search based on your specific medical needs and the doctor's availability.

  • What steps do I need to follow to book an appointment with a doctor?

    After selecting your preferred doctor, you can choose an available date and time that suits you. Once decided, confirm the booking to secure your appointment. It's that simple!

  • How can I rate and review a doctor after my appointment?

    Your feedback matters! After your appointment, effortlessly share your experience by navigating to your profile.
